# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 25 December 2025

Welcome to **ILrean**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information when you use our educational application. Our app is designed for children and general audiences and allows uploading, viewing, and interacting with educational content like PDFs, slides, and other study materials. AI services (Gemini) are integrated in the backend only.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Information You Voluntarily Provide:**  
- Name, email address, and any files you upload (e.g., slides, books, questions, or PDFs).  

**Usage Data:**  
- How you interact with the app, including pages visited, questions asked, tests created, and files viewed.  

**Technical Data:**  
- Device type, operating system, and connection information to improve your experience.  

**Media Data:**  
- Audio recordings (voice notes) created in the app are temporarily stored locally for sending and then uploaded to secure servers.  
- PDF files and other shared media are accessed via secure links from the backend and are not stored locally.  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to deliver and enhance our educational services, including:

- Analyzing and processing uploaded files.  
- Generating answers, summaries, and educational tests using backend AI (Gemini).  
- Personalizing your app experience based on preferences.  
- Sending notifications and study recommendations.  
- Temporarily storing and delivering audio recordings and PDF files in group chats.  

**Important:** Media files are not permanently saved on the device; they are accessed through secure links from our backend.  

---

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do **not sell or rent** your information. Data may be shared with:

- **Trusted service providers**, including cloud storage and AI processing services.  
- **Legal authorities** if required by law.  

**No advertising data or ad identifiers are collected.**

## 4. Data Security

We implement technical and administrative measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.  

Audio recordings and PDF files uploaded for sharing are stored securely and accessible only by intended users or group members.
