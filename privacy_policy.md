# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** 25 December 2025

Welcome to **ilrean**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information when you use our application, including any educational content you upload or interact with. Our app is designed for children and general audiences.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Information You Voluntarily Provide:**  
- Name, email address, and any files you upload (e.g., slides, books, questions, or PDFs for educational purposes).

**Usage Data:**  
- How you interact with the app, such as pages visited, questions asked, tests created, and files viewed.

**Technical Data:**  
- Device type, operating system, and connection information to provide a better user experience.

**Media Data:**  
- Audio recordings created in the app (e.g., voice notes) are temporarily stored locally for sending and then uploaded to our secure servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to deliver and improve our educational services, including:

- Analyzing and processing uploaded files.  
- Generating answers, summaries, and educational tests.  
- Personalizing the experience based on user preferences.  
- Sending smart notifications and study recommendations.  
- Temporarily storing and delivering audio recordings and PDF files in group chats.

---

## 3. Sharing Your Information
We do **not sell or rent** your information to third parties. We may share data with:

- **Trusted service providers** (e.g., cloud storage, AI services like Gemini in the backend).  
- **Legal authorities** when required by law.

No advertising data or ad identifiers are collected.

---

## 4. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Audio and PDF files uploaded for sharing in the app are stored securely and only accessible by the intended group members.

---

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to:

- Access your personal data.  
- Request correction or deletion of your information.  
- Delete your account at any time.
